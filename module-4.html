Module 04

Product &amp; service lines

By the end of this module you'll be able to
  • Explain how each line — bulk, mobile fleet fueling, emergency, tank sales & leasing, DEF, aviation — actually earns money.
  • Compare them on margin, volume, and operational complexity.
  • See why premium/emergency work and tank leasing change the margin math from Module 3.

Each service line has a different margin and operational profile — emergency work earns a premium for reliability, tank leasing creates recurring revenue and locks in the fuel supply, DEF rides alongside diesel. Full module released as the live course reaches it.
