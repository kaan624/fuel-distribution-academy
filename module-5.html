Module 05

Pricing &amp; bid strategy

By the end of this module you'll be able to
  • Read OPIS / rack pricing and the NY vs NJ spread the way a buyer does.
  • Build a defensible quote: cost basis + delivery cost + target CPG + risk.
  • Understand how competitive bids and RFPs (e.g. transit, municipal) are won without giving away margin.

Pricing ties together the rack mechanics from Module 2 and the unit economics from Module 3 into a number you hand a customer. Full module released as the live course reaches it.
