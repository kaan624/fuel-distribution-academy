Module 06

Customers &amp; segments

By the end of this module you'll be able to
  • Profile each segment — construction, logistics, rail, municipal, marine, aviation — by buying behavior and contract type.
  • Map account lifecycles, especially the construction ramp-and-taper.
  • Connect segment mix to the volume and density that drive profit.

Different segments buy differently — municipalities bid on OPIS-indexed contracts, construction accounts ramp then disappear, fleets want predictable on-site fueling. Full module released as the live course reaches it.
