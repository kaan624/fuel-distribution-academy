Module 07

Regulation &amp; compliance, practically

By the end of this module you'll be able to
  • Navigate EPA / environmental rules that touch a distributor day to day.
  • Understand Hazmat transport requirements for moving fuel.
  • Handle fuel/excise taxes and IFTA, plus tank and SPCC obligations.

Compliance is operational, not abstract: every gallon carries taxes, every truck carries Hazmat obligations, every tank carries spill-plan duties. Full module released as the live course reaches it.
